General Education

Bachelor of Elementary Education

General Education courses are the cornerstone of any well-rounded academic expression. They provide students with a broad foundation of knowledge and skills across diverse disciplines, fostering critical thinking and its core components. General Education courses are not merely elective credits; they are essential for developing a holistic understanding of the world and its complexities. They expose students to different perspectives, cultivate intellectual curiosity, and equip them with the tools necessary to navigate a changing society.
